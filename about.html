About

The Quantum SHIELD Laboratory is supervised by Dr. Betis Baheri in the Department of Cybersecurity at SUNY Canton, NY. The aim of this labratory is to conduct research in the field of quantum computing, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. Betis Baheri, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Cybersecurity at SUNY Canton and the founder of the Quantum Shield Laboratory (QSL), specializes in quantum computing, high-performance computing, machine learning, and cybersecurity. He honed his experience in large-scale computational systems at the Texas Advanced Computing Center and has collaborated with Los Alamos and Pacific Northwest National Laboratories. Baheri received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Kent State University.
